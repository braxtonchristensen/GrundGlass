About Us

I started my lampworking career by attending a few soft glass classes, making simple flowers and slowly moved on to making more intricate pieces such as vases and platters. From there I moved on to lampworking, I flew out to CA to study under Revere Glass for a couple weeks learning how to make simple spoons, dabbers, hammers, Sherlocks and bubblers. I flew back to Utah and got my own studio set up by converting a 5th wheel trailer. Everyday I try to push my skills to make the best glass possible for you guys.
